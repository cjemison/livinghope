What we do

Call to Worship

It's all too easy to simply come to church and mentally, emotionally, and spiritually check out. We believe attending a church service is more like a family gathering than watching a movie so our "Call to Worship" is always meant to draw us back into community with each other and with God and to encourage us to consider why we worship.

Praise and Worship

This is a slight misnomer. At Living Hope we believe that praise and worship are more than just songs we sing together but rather how we live our lives. We believe that we can worship and praise God through our occupations, our relationships, in addition to our voices and songs. At the same time, we believe it's important to come together corporately to sing to God and each other what He has done for us and who He is. Perhaps the best way to describe our typical musical style would be "Hillsong mixed with time-tested hymns".

Missions Updates

We believe we are called to preach the Gospel to the ends of the earth. One way we try to encourage growth in this area is in giving monthly updates on various missionaries we are supporting and by praying together for the persecuted church in different countries. See the missions page for more details.

Message

Just as we worship through singing and music, we also worship through the hearing of God's word preached to us. We believe that God's word is living and active meaning that God is actively speaking to us through it into our individual circumstances and hearts.

Prayer and Response

After hearing a message, this is an opportunity to let the truths of who God is and what He's done for us "soak in". This is also a good opportunity to respond to all of that in prayer.

Offering

Often a sensitive topic, we believe that everything we own has been provided and entrusted to us by God. We are merely stewards of the gifts that He's given so, also as an act of worship, we offer it back to Him. If you consider Living Hope to be your home church, we encourage you to participate in this way. However, if you are visiting or checking us out, please don't feel obligated in any way to give.

Praise and Worship

We typically bookend the service with musical worship as a way to emphasize that the whole service, not just the singing, is worship. For more information on "Praise and Worship", see above.

Announcements

Receive the latest news on upcoming events, new ways to get involved, and everything in between!

Fellowship and Covenantal Meal

After each service, we share a meal together knowing that food and community come hand in hand with each other. Please join us!
