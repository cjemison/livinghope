Missions

We want to be a church that is growing in seeing and participating in God’s purposes around the world. Part of our mission is to reach the local community through outreach and support campus ministries and global missions. Missions Sunday happens at the end of each month where we get an opportunity to take part in supporting missions through our prayers and resources. We hope this can challenge us to grow in our understanding of God’s mission and teach us to steward our resources well so that the name of Jesus would be proclaimed in the city of Glendale Heights, to the northwest suburbs of Chicago, and to the ends of the earth!
