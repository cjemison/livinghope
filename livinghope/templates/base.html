About

Living Hope is a Bible-believing, Jesus-loving, Gospel-preaching church in the west suburbs of Chicago. Our desire is to be continually journeying together towards living out the Gospel more and more in our lives, community, and mission. If this sounds interesting to you, join us! We'd love to have you!
